About embARC

The embARC Community is a comprehensive resource for embedded developers, providing a single point of access to Free and Open Source Software (FOSS), Tools and Platforms to accelerate the development of embedded applications for ARC Processors.

The embARC Community is actively managed by Synopsys and contributions from the broader community are welcomed. Users are encouraged to contribute support for new packages, submit enhancements requests and/or contributions to existing projects as well as bug reports and bug fixes.

Please consult each individual project for applicable contribution guidelines.
