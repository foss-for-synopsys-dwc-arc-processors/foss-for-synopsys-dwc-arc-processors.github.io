embARC Board Support Package (BSP)

The embARC Board support Package (BSP) for DesignWare® ARC® EM Starter Kit (EMSK) is a software distribution aimed at facilitating the development and evaluation of embedded systems based on ARCv2 processors. It is designed to provide a board support package for ARC EMSK users by defining consistent and simple software interfaces to the processors and onboard devices.
